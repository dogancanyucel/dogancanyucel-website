Cross-Platform Mobile App

Turquoise AI: Calorie & Fitness

AI Calorie & Fitness Coach

📱 Android & iOS — One App

Turquoise is a single product across mobile platforms — the same AI coaching engine, nutrition tools, and fitness tracking experience on Android and iOS. Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose on Android; SwiftUI and HealthKit on iOS.

One App, Two Platforms

In Development

Android

Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Google Gemini, Firebase, Health Connect, home-screen widgets, and tiered AI daily quotas. Active development and testing.

Roadmap

iOS

Same feature set with SwiftUI and HealthKit — shared backend, shared AI logic, and one unified Turquoise experience for Apple users.

Key Features

💬

AI Chat Coach

Personalized fitness advice, program updates, and macro guidance.

📸

Food Photo Analysis

Gemini-powered calorie and macro estimates from meal photos.

🎙️

Voice Meal Logging

Hands-free meal logging with AI parsing.

🏋️

Smart Gym Tracker

Set tracking, rest timers, and AI-calculated burned calories.

🫀

Body Scan

AI body composition and posture feedback — photos never stored.

📊

Weekly AI Reports

Automated progress summaries and plan adjustments.

Tech Stack

Kotlin · Jetpack Compose SwiftUI · HealthKit Google Gemini AI Firebase / Firestore Room / SQLite Cloudflare Workers
