Featured App

Turquoise AI: Calorie & Fitness

AI Calorie & Fitness Coach

🤖 Android — Active 🍎 iOS — Coming Soon

Turquoise is a fully personalized AI fitness companion that builds custom gym programs, tracks nutrition with photo and voice logging, analyzes body composition, and coaches you through every rep — powered by Google Gemini on Android, with an Apple release on the roadmap.

Platform Roadmap

Available Now

Android

Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. Includes AI chat coach, food photo analysis, voice meal logging, gym tracker with smart calorie burn, body scan, weekly AI reports, home-screen widgets, Firebase sync, and tiered AI daily quotas (Trial / Premium).

In Planning

iOS

The Apple version is on the roadmap with feature parity in mind — SwiftUI interface, HealthKit integration, and the same AI coaching engine. Follow updates on this page or contact me for early access interest.

Key Features

💬

AI Chat Coach

Conversational fitness assistant with personalized advice, program updates, and macro guidance.

📸

Food Photo Analysis

Snap a meal photo — Gemini estimates calories and macros instantly.

🎙️

Voice Meal Logging

Log meals hands-free with voice input and AI parsing.

🏋️

Smart Gym Tracker

Interactive set tracking, rest timers, and AI-calculated burned calories per exercise.

🫀

Body Scan

AI body composition analysis with posture feedback and calorie recommendations.

📊

Weekly AI Reports

Automated weekly fitness summaries with progress insights and plan adjustments.
