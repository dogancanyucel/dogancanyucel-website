Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to turquoise_-ai-calorie-&-fitness ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and health-related data is handled securely and transparently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application on iOS and Android platforms, including any associated services such as Apple Watch integration (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use our Service, you may provide us with certain personal data, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and subscription details (Premium €9.99/month).
  • Text Inputs: Search history for food logging and interactions with our AI Chat feature.
  • Physical Characteristics: Basic demographic and biometric data you manually enter to help us calculate your fitness metrics.

2.2 Health Connect Data (Android)

With your explicit consent, we request access to read specific health and fitness data from Google Health Connect:

  • StepsRecord: Your daily step count.
  • ActiveCaloriesBurned: Calories burned during activities.
  • DistanceRecord: Distance covered during your physical activities.

2.3 Apple HealthKit & Apple Watch Data (iOS)

With your explicit permission, we access and read data from Apple HealthKit and your Apple Watch:

  • Step Count: (HKQuantityTypeIdentifier.stepCount)
  • Active Energy Burned: (Active Calories)
  • Walking + Running Distance: (Distance)
  • Heart Rate & Heart Rate Zones: To monitor intensity during workouts.
  • Running Dynamics: For advanced form and performance analysis.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve the core functionalities of the Service. Specifically:

  • Health and Fitness Metrics: Health Connect and HealthKit data are used solely to generate your "AI Weekly Report," provide real-time feedback during workouts, and construct highly personalized diet and training plans.
  • AI Analytics: To power our artificial intelligence engine which offers fitness insights, calculates nutritional needs, and tracks progress.
  • Service Provisioning: To manage your account, process subscription payments, and communicate with you regarding your service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Strict Limited Use Policy for Health Data

We absolutely do NOT sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal health data to any advertising networks, data brokers, or any other third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

The use of information received from Google Health Connect and Apple HealthKit will adhere strictly to their respective limited use policies. Your health data is accessed and utilized solely to provide the core fitness, health, and AI analysis features of the application.

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share non-health information with trusted third-party service providers (such as payment processors and cloud hosting providers) only to the extent necessary to operate the Service. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any other purposes.

5. Ephemeral Processing of Media (Photos and Audio)

Our app utilizes advanced AI (via the Gemini API) to analyze your meals and posture, as well as to transcribe voice commands.

  • Photos: Body photos uploaded for posture analysis and meal photos uploaded for calorie calculation are processed ephemerally on our servers.
  • Audio: Voice recordings captured via the voice button for food logging are transcribed into text instantly.

Crucially, all such image and audio files are permanently and immediately deleted from our servers the moment the AI analysis or transcription is complete. We do not store, log, or use these media files for training AI models.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Your Rights and Control:

  • Revoking Access: You maintain full control over your health data. You can revoke our access to Google Health Connect or Apple HealthKit at any time directly through your device settings.
  • Account Deletion (Data Deletion Request): You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated historical data at any time directly within the application with a single click. Upon initiating this request, all your personal information, historical fitness data, and chat logs will be irrevocably erased from our active databases.

7. Security of Your Data

We employ industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Medical Disclaimer

The turquoise_-ai-calorie-&-fitness application is NOT a medical device. The AI-generated recommendations, nutritional plans, training programs, and any other information provided by the Service are for informational and educational purposes only. They are not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. You should not use the information provided by the app to make clinical decisions or replace a doctor's prescription. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition before starting any new diet or fitness program.
