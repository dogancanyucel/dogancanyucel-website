PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Turquoise AI Calorie & Fitness. Dogancan Yucel is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and health-related data is handled securely and transparently. This Privacy Policy explains how Dogancan Yucel collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use the mobile application (the "App") on iOS and Android platforms, including any associated services such as Apple Watch integration (collectively, the "Service").

Please note that the website dogancanyucel.com is used solely as an informational landing page and host for these legal documents. The website itself does not collect, track, or store any of your personal data.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collected Within the App

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use the Service, you may provide certain personal data, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and subscription details (Premium €9.99/month).
  • Text Inputs: Search history for food logging and interactions with the AI Chat feature.
  • Physical Characteristics: Basic demographic and biometric data you manually enter (such as current weight, target weight, height, and date of birth) to help calculate your fitness metrics.

2.2 Google Health Connect Data (Android)

With your explicit consent, the App requests access to read specific health and fitness data from Google Health Connect:

  • StepsRecord: Your daily step count.
  • ActiveCaloriesBurned: Calories burned during activities.
  • DistanceRecord: Distance covered during your physical activities.

2.3 Apple HealthKit (iOS)

With your explicit permission, the App accesses and reads data from Apple HealthKit and your Apple Watch:

  • Step Count: (HKQuantityTypeIdentifier.stepCount)
  • Active Energy Burned: Active Calories (HKQuantityTypeIdentifier.activeEnergyBurned)
  • Walking + Running Distance: Distance (HKQuantityTypeIdentifier.distanceWalkingRunning)
  • Heart Rate & Heart Rate Zones: To monitor intensity during workouts (HKQuantityTypeIdentifier.heartRate)
  • Running Dynamics: For advanced form and performance analysis.

3. How Your Information Is Used

Dogancan Yucel uses the collected information exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve the core functionalities of the Service. Specifically:

  • Health and Fitness Metrics: Health Connect and HealthKit data are used solely to generate your "AI Weekly Report," provide real-time feedback during workouts on your Apple Watch, and construct highly personalized diet and training plans.
  • AI Analytics: To power the artificial intelligence engine which offers fitness insights, calculates nutritional needs, and tracks progress.
  • Service Provisioning: To manage your account, process subscription payments, and communicate with you regarding your service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Strict Limited Use Policy for Health Data

Dogancan Yucel absolutely does NOT sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal health data to any advertising networks, data brokers, or any other third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

The use of information received from Google Health Connect and Apple HealthKit will adhere strictly to their respective limited use policies. Your health data is accessed and utilized solely to provide the core fitness, health, and AI analysis features of the application.

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

Dogancan Yucel may share non-health information with trusted third-party service providers (such as payment processors and cloud hosting providers) only to the extent necessary to operate the Service. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any other purposes.

5. Ephemeral Processing of Media (Photos and Audio)

The App utilizes advanced AI (via the Gemini API) to analyze your meals and posture, as well as to transcribe voice commands.

  • Photos: Body photos uploaded for posture analysis and meal photos uploaded for calorie calculation are processed ephemerally (in-memory) during transit via the Gemini API.
  • Audio: Voice recordings captured via the voice button for food logging are transcribed into text instantly.

CRITICAL PRIVACY GUARANTEE: All such image and audio files are permanently and immediately deleted the moment the AI analysis or transcription is complete. These media files are never stored, logged, or saved on Firebase, any server, or third-party cloud storage, nor are they used for training AI models.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your structured database data (profile, fitness logs, and AI chat history) is retained only for as long as your App account is active.

Your Rights and Control:

  • Revoking Access: You maintain full control over your health data. You can revoke access to Google Health Connect or Apple HealthKit at any time directly through your device settings.
  • Account Deletion (Data Deletion Request): You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated historical data at any time directly within the application with a single click. Upon initiating this request, all your personal information, historical fitness logs, and chat history will be irrevocably erased from the Firebase databases. You may also request deletion by contacting yuceldogancan0@gmail.com.

7. Security of Your Data

Industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, are employed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While commercially acceptable means are used to protect your data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Medical Disclaimer

THE TURQUOISE AI CALORIE & FITNESS APPLICATION IS NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE.

The AI-generated recommendations, nutritional plans, training programs, posture assessments, and any other information provided by the Service are for informational and educational purposes only. They are not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. You should not use the information provided by the app to make clinical decisions or replace a doctor's prescription. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition before starting any new diet or fitness program.
