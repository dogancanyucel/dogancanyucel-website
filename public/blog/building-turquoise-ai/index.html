Building Turquoise AI: Calorie & Fitness

Turquoise AI: Calorie & Fitness started as a simple question: what if your phone could act as a real personal trainer and nutritionist — not generic templates, but programs shaped around your body, goals, and daily habits?

Android first

The Android app is built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. Core features include:

  • AI chat coach powered by Google Gemini
  • Food photo analysis and voice meal logging
  • Smart gym tracker with AI-calculated burned calories
  • Body scan with ephemeral photo processing (never stored)
  • Weekly AI fitness reports
  • Home-screen widgets for steps, water, and activity

Backend & sync

User profiles, AI usage counters, and trial/premium state sync through Firebase Firestore. Exercise data is served via a Cloudflare Worker with D1 database on dogancanyucel.com — the same domain that hosts this blog, legal pages, and the project page.

Fair AI usage

AI calls are expensive at scale. Turquoise implements tiered quotas — Free (trial expired), Trial (7-day limits), and Premium — with a daily general cap and smart chat adjustment when daily feature limits exceed the general pool. More on that in the AI quota design post.

iOS next

Android is live in active development. The Apple version is planned with SwiftUI, HealthKit, and feature parity with the Android coaching engine. Follow this blog for iOS progress updates.
